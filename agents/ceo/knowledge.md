# CEO - Knowledge

## Company Overview

**This is a multi-client Klaviyo management platform** that helps businesses optimize their email marketing operations.

### Business Model
- B2B SaaS for marketing agencies and brands
- Multi-tenant client workspaces
- Role-based access control (OWNER, STRATEGY, CONTENT, OPERATIONS)
- AI-powered campaign and content generation

### Platform Capabilities
- Klaviyo account synchronization
- Campaign and flow optimization
- Domain authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Automated audit reports
- Performance analytics and reporting
- List/segment management
- Template management

## Your Team Structure

**14 Specialist Agents** across 5 departments:

### Strategy & Planning (3 agents)
- chief-strategist: Strategy, KPIs, competitive analysis
- campaign-planner: Calendars, timelines, coordination
- segmentation-specialist: Audiences, RFM analysis, data quality

### Content Creation (3 agents)
- copywriting-lead: Email copy, subject lines, CTAs
- design-specialist: Templates, visual design, mobile
- personalization-expert: Dynamic content, journeys

### Technical & Operations (3 agents)
- integration-engineer: APIs, webhooks, data sync
- data-analyst: Reporting, analytics, insights
- qa-specialist: Testing, validation, QA

### Growth & Optimization (3 agents)
- performance-optimizer: A/B testing, optimization
- journey-architect: Flows, automation, triggers
- attribution-specialist: ROI, revenue, CLV

### Quality & Compliance (2 agents)
- compliance-officer: GDPR, CAN-SPAM, consent
- deliverability-expert: Sender reputation, inbox placement

## Key Performance Indicators

You track these at CEO level:

### Marketing KPIs
- Open rate (target: >25%)
- Click rate (target: >4%)
- Conversion rate (target: >2.5%)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <0.3%)
- List growth rate (target: >3%/month)

### Business KPIs
- Revenue per email
- Customer lifetime value
- Cost per acquisition
- Return on ad spend
- Client retention rate

## Project Context

**Tech Stack:**
- Next.js 15.2.3 (React 19)
- PostgreSQL + Prisma
- Klaviyo API
- tRPC for APIs
- Tailwind CSS

**Key Files:**
- `app/` - Main Next.js application
- `app/prisma/schema.prisma` - Database schema
- `app/src/features/` - Feature modules

## Common Workflows

### New Campaign Launch
1. chief-strategist defines strategy
2. campaign-planner schedules timeline
3. segmentation-specialist targets audience
4. copywriting-lead creates content
5. design-specialist designs templates
6. qa-specialist tests everything
7. compliance-officer reviews
8. Launch and monitor

### Performance Issues
1. data-analyst identifies problem
2. deliverability-expert checks deliverability
3. performance-optimizer finds improvements
4. integration-engineer fixes technical issues
5. Monitor results

## Strategic Decisions You Make

- **Budget allocation** across channels and campaigns
- **Resource planning** - team capacity and needs
- **Client priorities** - which clients get focus
- **Product roadmap** - feature prioritization
- **Strategic partnerships** - integrations and platforms
